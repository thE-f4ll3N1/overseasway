Cancellation and Refund Policy for Overseas Way

We strive to ensure your satisfaction with our products. Please review our cancellation and refund policy below:

Cancellations

  • Digital Products: Once an eBook is purchased and delivered, it cannot be canceled as it is a digital product.
  • Subscription Services: Subscriptions can be canceled at any time, but no refunds will be provided for the remaining period of the subscription.

Refunds

  • EBooks: Due to the nature of digital products, we do not offer refunds for eBooks once they have been downloaded or accessed.

Exchanges

  • Digital Products: If you experience any issues with your eBook, please contact us, and we will assist you in resolving the issue.

Contact Us

For any questions regarding cancellations or refunds, please contact us at support@overseasway.in.
